30 messages into a super focused discussion with Claude it often brings up loads of good points that I'd like to explore further.
However I want to be able to explore an idea super briefly **without** affecting the existing chat history or Claude's fragile context.

Even when an LLM is super prime I feel like I want to save this specific primed LLM and use it in the future. You can say oh you can do that with agents, system prompts, mcp servers, context management. But its not on the fly!

Options:
1. Continue in the main chat, could possibly effect the existing context and when you have Claude/Gemini super locked in and focused the last thing you want to do to its impressionable sensitive brain is pollute it with other possibilities. IYKYK.
2. Spin up a side chat, paste some context from the main chat, brief conversation -> output. Done, close chat. Even easier with Gemini's temporary chats that don't save to your chat history.
3. In Antigravity/Cursor I often replicate a branch by discussing this tangential idea in the main chat then once I'm satisfied I scroll back up to the tangential branch message and click "Undo changes up to this point".
